Responsible Social Media: The Do's

1.Audit Privacy Settings: Regularly check your account settings to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.

2.Build a Professional Brand: Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your latest academic projects, skills, and certifications.

3.Practice the "Pause" Rule: Take 30 seconds to think before posting a heated comment or controversial opinion.

4.Verify Before Sharing: Fact-check news stories and sources before reposting to prevent the spread of misinformation.

5.Curate a Positive Feed: Follow accounts that provide educational value, inspiration, or career-related networking.



Responsible Social Media: The Don'ts

1.Overshare Sensitive Info: Never post your precise real-time location, home address, or personal ID numbers.

2.Post Incriminating Content: Avoid sharing photos or videos of illegal activities or unprofessional behavior.

3.Engage in "Cyber-Venting": Don't post negative rants about professors, classmates, or current/past employers.

4.Trust Viral "Rage-Bait": Avoid interacting with or spreading inflammatory content designed only to trigger emotional reactions.

5.Compare Your Life to Others: Don't tie your self-worth to likes, views, or the "highlight reels" of other people's lives.
